About hamsters

Hamsters are rodents (order Rodentia) belonging to the subfamily Cricetinae, which contains 19 species categorised in seven genera.They have become set up as current little pets.The best-known species of hamster is the golden or Syrian hamster (Mesocricetus auratus), which is the write the vast majority of often kept as pets. Other hamster species usually kept as pets are the three species of petite hamster, Campbell's petite hamster (Phodopus campbelli), the winter white petite hamster (Phodopus sungorus) and the Roborovski hamster (Phodopus roborovskii).
